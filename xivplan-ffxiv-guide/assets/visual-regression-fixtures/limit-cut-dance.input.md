# Limit Cut Dance

P1 00:16 players receive numbers 1-8. Odd numbers prepare north, even numbers prepare south, and the boss remains center.

P1 00:24 numbers 1 and 2 bait line attacks and ground fire. Other players wait on their half of the arena.

P1 00:32 numbers 3 and 4 take over the bait positions while 1 and 2 rotate out along the edge. Continue the sequence for 5-8, then reset to eight-way positions.
