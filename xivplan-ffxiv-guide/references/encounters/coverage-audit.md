# Encounter Coverage Audit

## Scope

This audit records Phase 3 retrieval coverage. `floor-indexed` means every Savage floor has at least one high-value mechanic-cluster card that can answer analogy queries. It does not mean every raidwide, auto-attack, cast name, or regional macro variation has its own card.

The intended maintenance rule is:

1. Index every floor with at least one transferable mechanic cluster.
2. Add extra cards for mechanics commonly used as analogies.
3. Preserve unknowns and strategy-variant warnings.
4. Refresh live-tier Arcadion cards before drawing concrete strategies.
