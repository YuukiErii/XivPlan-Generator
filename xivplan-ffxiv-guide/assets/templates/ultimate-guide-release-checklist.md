# Ultimate Guide Release Checklist

## v0.1-draft

- [ ] Raw notes include source and confidence.
- [ ] Unknowns are explicit in guide and risk report.
- [ ] Candidate report compares at least three strategies.
- [ ] `.xivplan`, PNG, Markdown, DOCX, and PDF are generated.
- [ ] Quality gate passes.

## v0.2-observed

- [ ] New observed information is listed.
- [ ] Changed assumptions are recorded in `change-log.md`.
- [ ] Old version artifacts are retained.
- [ ] Guide wording distinguishes confirmed facts from pending assumptions.

## v0.3-stabilized

- [ ] Standing positions and movement routes are stable.
- [ ] Candidate recommendation no longer depends on blocking unknowns.
- [ ] Common mistakes reflect observed wipes.

## v1.0-release

- [ ] All blocking unknowns are resolved or explicitly scoped out.
- [ ] Public text avoids speculative claims.
- [ ] `.xivplan` opens and each step image is readable.
- [ ] Markdown, DOCX, and PDF are present and non-empty.
